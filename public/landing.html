How SocialChef Works

From a simple prompt to published content — see the smart creation pipeline in action.

Step 1: AI-Powered Suggestions

SocialChef proactively creates post ideas based on your brand voice, audience demographics, and trending topics in your industry. The AI analyzes what content performs best for businesses like yours and generates fresh ideas daily.

Step 2: Smart Content Generation

SocialChef writes copy, selects images, and crafts platform-optimized posts. The AI handles everything from captions and hashtags to image selection and video concepts, all matching your brand's unique tone and style.

Step 3: Multi-Platform Adaptation

Content is automatically tailored for each platform's format and best practices. A single piece of content becomes a feed post for Instagram, a carousel for LinkedIn, a reel for TikTok, and a story for Facebook — each optimized for that platform's audience and algorithm.

Step 4: Schedule & Publish

Any platform, any time. Schedule feed posts, reels, stories, carousels and more — or let SocialChef pick the best time for your audience. The AI analyzes when your followers are most active and publishes at optimal times for maximum engagement.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SocialChef?

SocialChef is an AI-powered social media management platform designed for restaurants, local service businesses, and growing startups. It automates the entire content lifecycle — from generating post ideas based on your brand voice and trending topics, to writing copy, creating visuals, and publishing across Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, X, LinkedIn, and Pinterest.

What social media platforms does SocialChef support?

SocialChef supports 6 major platforms: Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, X (formerly Twitter), LinkedIn, and Pinterest. Content is automatically adapted for each platform's format, including feed posts, reels, stories, carousels, and text posts.

How does SocialChef create content?

SocialChef uses AI to generate content that matches your brand voice. You can chat with the AI assistant to request specific content, or let SocialChef proactively suggest post ideas based on your brand, audience demographics, and trending topics. The AI writes copy, selects or generates images, adds hashtags, and optimizes everything for each platform.

Can I manage multiple brands with SocialChef?

Yes. SocialChef supports multi-brand management from a single dashboard. This is ideal for agencies, franchise owners, or entrepreneurs running multiple businesses. Each brand maintains its own voice, visual identity, and posting schedule.

Does SocialChef run paid ads?

Yes. The built-in Ad Campaign Manager lets you create and manage Meta (Facebook and Instagram), Google, and Pinterest ad campaigns directly from the app. Features include AI-powered copywriting, targeting optimization, budget management, and automated performance reporting with ROAS, CPC, and conversion tracking.
